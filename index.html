Foletto is a DJ and music producer. His journey in electronic music is unique. With major official releases, the Brazilian artist has already caught the attention of renowned figures in the industry, such as Guy Mantzur, D-Nox, Hernán Cattáneo, Dosem, and Facundo Mohrr, among others.

As a resident of Sonido Profundo and Radiola Records, the Curitiba native has released his music through internationally acclaimed labels like Proton, Natura Viva, Stripped Recordings, and Warung Recordings.
